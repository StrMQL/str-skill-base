# Author and Maintenance

**English** | [中文](zh/author.md)

## Maintenance contract

Skill Base is maintained by a **part-time independent developer** as a side project.

- **Issues and pull requests are welcome.**
- Responses and reviews are concentrated on **weekday evenings and weekends**, not business-hour SLAs.
- Large features may wait until they align with maintainer capacity; small fixes and clear PRs move faster.

That is intentional. The project favors sustainable pace over pretend-on-call support.
